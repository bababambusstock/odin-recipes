Odin Recipes - Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g chickpea noodles
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the chickpea noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the can of chopped tomatoes, and add the dried oregano and basil. Stir well to combine.
  5. Let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Once the sauce has thickened, add the cooked chickpea noodles to the pan and toss to coat them evenly with the sauce.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil if desired.
